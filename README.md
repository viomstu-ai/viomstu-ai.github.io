Privacy Policy for MorrowFernStudio Pinterest Integration

Effective date: 22 July 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the MorrowFernStudio Pinterest Integration processes information when connecting to Pinterest through the Pinterest API.

The integration is operated by MorrowFernStudio in Denmark.

The integration is an internal tool used solely by the operator of MorrowFernStudio. It is not offered to customers, members of the public, or other Pinterest users.

2. Data controller

The data controller is:

MorrowFernStudio
Trading as MorrowFernStudio
Denmark


3. Information accessed

The integration only accesses information connected to the operator’s own Pinterest account.

Depending on the Pinterest permissions granted to the integration, this information may include:

• Pinterest account identifiers
• Pinterest profile information
• Boards and board identifiers
• Pins and Pin identifiers
• Pin descriptions, links, images and related metadata
• Account or content analytics, where an analytics permission has been granted
• OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens used to authenticate API requests

The integration does not allow other people to connect their Pinterest accounts.

4. How information is used

Pinterest account information is used only to operate and manage the MorrowFernStudio Pinterest account.

This may include:

• Creating and publishing Pins
• Selecting or managing Pinterest boards
• Updating Pin information
• Retrieving information needed to confirm that content was published
• Reviewing performance data where the relevant Pinterest permission has been granted
• Maintaining and securing the connection between the integration and Pinterest

Information is not used for advertising profiling, data brokerage, or marketing to third parties.

5. Legal basis

Where the General Data Protection Regulation applies, information is processed on the basis of the legitimate interests of MorrowFernStudio in operating, maintaining and securing its own Pinterest account and internal publishing workflow.

Pinterest access can be withdrawn by revoking the integration’s authorization through the relevant Pinterest account settings.

6. Storage and retention

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens may be stored securely for as long as they are required to maintain the Pinterest connection.

Pinterest account information is requested from the Pinterest API when needed. The integration does not intentionally maintain a separate permanent database containing copies of Pinterest account content unless storage is required for the operation of a specific permitted feature.

Temporary information, technical logs and error records may be retained for the limited period required to operate, secure and troubleshoot the integration.

Information is deleted or anonymised when it is no longer required, when the integration is discontinued, or when Pinterest authorization is revoked, subject to any applicable legal or security requirements.

7. Sharing of information

Pinterest account information is not sold.

Information may be processed by:

• Pinterest, which provides the Pinterest platform and API
• Hosting or infrastructure providers used to operate the integration
• Technical service providers used to secure, maintain or troubleshoot the integration
• Public authorities where disclosure is required by law

Service providers only receive information needed to provide their services.

Pinterest processes information under its own privacy policy and terms.

8. International transfers

Pinterest and technical service providers may process information outside Denmark or the European Economic Area.

Where legally required, appropriate safeguards should be used for international transfers, including contractual protections or other transfer mechanisms recognised under applicable data-protection law.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect account information and OAuth credentials.

These measures may include restricted access, encrypted connections, secure secret storage and limiting Pinterest permissions to those required for the integration’s functions.

No method of electronic storage or transmission can guarantee complete security.

10. Cookies and analytics

The integration does not use advertising cookies or behavioural advertising technologies.

The privacy-policy webpage or hosting provider may process basic technical information, such as an IP address, browser type, request time and server logs, for security and delivery purposes.

The integration does not use third-party audience analytics unless this policy is updated to disclose that processing.

11. Data-protection rights

Where applicable, individuals have rights concerning their personal information, including the rights to:

• Request access to their personal information
• Request correction of inaccurate information
• Request deletion of information
• Request restriction of processing
• Object to processing based on legitimate interests
• Request data portability where applicable
• Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
• Lodge a complaint with a competent data-protection authority

Because the integration is used solely by its operator, it does not ordinarily process the personal information of external users.

Requests can be submitted to viomstu@gmail.com

12. Children’s information

The integration is an internal business tool. It is not directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the integration, its Pinterest permissions, its hosting arrangements, or applicable legal requirements change.

The effective date at the top of the policy will be updated when material changes are made.
